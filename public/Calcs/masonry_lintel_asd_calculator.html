Engineering Notes:
  • Design per TMS 402-22 ASD. Enter ASD load combinations per ASCE/SEI 7-22 §2.4 (e.g., D+0.7S for downward; 0.6D+0.6W for uplift).
  • Effective span per TMS 402-22 §5.3.1.1: L = clear opening + bearing/12 (ft). Minimum bearing = 4 in. per §5.3.1.4; 8 in. is typical.
  • Shear uses γg = 1.0 for lintels per §8.3.5.1.2 (lintels are not classified as partially grouted shear walls). Axial P = 0 assumed in shear equations.
  • ASD flexure: compressive reinforcement is ignored (not laterally tied). Governing moment is the lesser of Ms (steel controls) and Mm (masonry controls).
  • Self-weight of lintel + weight of masonry above opening must be included in w. Typical fully-grouted 8" CMU lintel: ~81 psf. Shear may be reduced to d/2 from face of support per §5.3.1.5.
  • Uplift check: if the net midspan moment under the uplift combination is negative (tension in top), top bars are required. Top bars are typically placed in a bond beam at the roof diaphragm level.
