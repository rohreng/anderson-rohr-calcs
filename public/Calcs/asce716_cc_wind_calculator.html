Engineering Notes:
  • Sign convention: p(+) acts inward (compression on surface); p(−) acts outward (suction). Both are checked against connections and cladding.
  • GCp interpolation: Values are log-linearly interpolated per the curve in each figure for the entered effective wind area A (clamped to 10–500 sf).
  • Velocity pressure: qh = 0.00256 × Kh × Kzt × Kd × V² (ASCE 7-16 Eq. 26.10-1). Kh uses the lower-bound table value for h (conservative).
  • Minimum pressure: ASCE 7-16 §30.2.2 requires C&C pressures not less than +16 / −16 psf net. Verify this minimum independently for cladding elements.
  • Overhangs: GCp values for overhangs act on the bottom surface and are additive to the top-surface pressure for the same zone. Not combined here — apply separately per Fig. notes.
  • This calculator does not substitute for engineering judgment. Verify figure applicability (roof slope, h limits) before using pressures in design.
