Engineering Notes:
  • Calculator transfers Mu to a flange-force couple Puf = Mu/(d − tf) and checks the chord-wall local yielding limit state (AISC 360-22 Eq. K1-7), which governs DG24 Example 4.3.
  • Limits of applicability per §K1.3 (Table K1.3A) must be satisfied: 0.25 ≤ bp/B ≤ 1.0, B/t ≤ 35, Fy ≤ 52 ksi.
  • Beam flange must fit on the flat of the HSS wall: bf ≤ B − 3t.
  • Web local yielding/crippling, weld design, beam shear connection, and HSS column compression checks are NOT included — perform separately.
  • Verified against DG24 Example 4.3: W16×57 on HSS10×10×1/2 → φMn ≈ 88 kip-ft.
