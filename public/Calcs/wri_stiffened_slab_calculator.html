Engineering Notes & Assumptions:
  • WRI beam depth formula d = ∛(664 M lc / B) — empirical WRI constant for reinforced concrete grade beams; units: M in kip-ft, lc in ft, B in in, d in in
  • Flexure per ACI 318-19 §22.2 rectangular stress block; ϕ = 0.90 for tension-controlled sections
  • Same moment demand used for both top and bottom steel (WRI assumes both faces experience uplift/settlement reversals)
  • Deflection uses simplified uniform-load beam formula (5qL⁴/384EI); T-beam composite action and cracked section are not included — verify critical deflections with detailed analysis
  • Shear capacity of grade beams should be verified separately per ACI 318-19 §22.5
  • Minimum steel per ACI 318-19 §9.6 and bar spacing per §25.2 must be confirmed for final design
  • This calculator implements the WRI Design Guide procedure by Walter L. Snowden, P.E.
