Engineering Notes:
  • Strong-axis LTB uses AISC 360-22 §F11 slenderness parameter Lbd/t². Section F11 applies only when d/t ≥ 1 (strong axis orientation).
  • Weak-axis flexure assumes bending about the plate's minor axis (y-y). LTB does not apply; yielding always governs.
  • Compression uses §E3 with governing radius of gyration ry = t/√12 (minor axis of plate, governs when d > t).
  • Combined loading per §H1-1 applies when Pu/(φPn) ≥ 0.2; §H1-2 applies otherwise.
  • No shear, torsion, or bearing checks are included — evaluate separately per §G and §J if applicable.
  • All demands must come from structural analysis; this calculator does not compute demand from loading.
