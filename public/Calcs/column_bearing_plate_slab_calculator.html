Engineering Notes & Assumptions:
  • Bearing pressure limit per ACI 318-19 §22.8.3.2 using the conservative bearing factor (no area modification — plate on large slab, A2/A1 = 1.0). If concrete is confirmed to extend well beyond the plate in all directions, the bearing capacity may be increased by √(A2/A1) ≤ 2.0.
  • Plate bending per AISC Design Guide 1 (DG01). Cantilever projections: m = (N − 0.95d)/2, n = (B − 0.80bf)/2, n' = λ√(d·bf)/4. Governing cantilever l = max(m, n, n').
  • Effective slab depth assumed: deff = h − 1.75 in (1.5 in cover + 0.5 in for assumed #4 bar radius).
  • Two-way punching shear per ACI 318-19 §22.6.5.2 (three governing equations). Critical perimeter bo measured at d/2 from plate faces. No shear reinforcement assumed (conservative).
  • One-way shear critical section taken at deff from the effective column face (0.95d/2 in N-direction, 0.80bf/2 in B-direction), consistent with DG01 cantilever projections. Vc per ACI 318-19 §22.5.5.1 simplified (Vc = 2λ√f'c·bw·d).
  • This calculator checks the slab capacity; verify the column-to-plate weld/connection separately.
