Engineering Notes:
  • Load Path: Wall zone loads (plf × length = kips) are distributed proportionally to grade beam span length as equivalent uniform loads. Refine with point-load analysis for critical cases.
  • Load Conversion: Grade beam LRFD demands use 1.2DL + 1.6LL. Pier ASD service loads use unfactored DL + LL from grade beam reactions.
  • Grade Beams: Analyzed as simply supported spans. For continuous grade beams, results are conservative at supports and unconservative at mid-span.
  • Shear: ACI 318-19 §22.5.5.1 simplified shear (Table approach). If φVc < Vu, stirrups are required — design separately.
  • Pier Embedment: Minimum total depth = depth to bearing + minimum penetration + active penetration required. Round up to 0.5 ft increments in the field.
  • Belled Piers: End bearing on bell face area. Skin friction on shaft above bell only, beyond minimum penetration.
  • Stacked Calc Link: Use "Export to Foundation Design" in the Stacked Headers & Studs Calc to auto-populate Section A with lowest-floor accumulated wall loads.
