Engineering Notes:
  • Load Path: Header reactions are treated as equivalent uniform loads on grade beams, distributed proportionally to span length. Refine with point-load analysis for critical cases.
  • Load Conversion: Grade beam LRFD demands use 1.2DL + 1.6LL. Pier ASD service loads use unfactored DL + LL from grade beam reactions.
  • Grade Beams: Analyzed as simply supported spans. For continuous grade beams, results are conservative at supports and unconservative at mid-span. Apply appropriate continuity factors.
  • Shear: ACI 318-19 §22.5.5.1 simplified shear (no axial load). If φVc < Vu, stirrups required — design separately.
  • Pier Embedment: Minimum total depth = depth to bearing + minimum penetration + active penetration required. Round up to 0.5 ft increments in the field.
  • Belled Piers: End bearing computed on bell face area. Skin friction along shaft above bell only, beyond minimum penetration. Bell diameter must be geotechnically achievable.
  • Pier Reinforcement: As,min per ACI 318-19 §26.8.2.1 (ρ ≥ 0.5% Ag). Design uplift reinforcement separately per project geotechnical report.
  • All header checks follow NDS 2018 ASD. Verify species, moisture, and load duration factors per project conditions.
