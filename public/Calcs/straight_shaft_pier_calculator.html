Engineering Notes:
  • Bearing capacities are allowable (ASD) values from geotechnical report. Confirm geotechnical report allows ASD design values as input.
  • Skin friction is applied only over penetration beyond the minimum penetration requirement per standard practice.
  • Soil uplift force is computed as: Uplift Pressure × Perimeter × Uplift Depth. This is an applied load from expansive soil or hydrostatic pressure acting on the pier shaft.
  • Uplift steel is designed by ASD: As = Net Uplift / (0.6 × Fy). Confirm this approach with project geotechnical engineer for site-specific conditions.
  • Minimum reinforcement per ACI 318-19 §26.8.2.1: ρmin ≥ 0.005Ag for drilled piers. If pier acts in column action (Leff/D > 3), use ρmin ≥ 0.01Ag per ACI 318-19 §10.6.1.1.
  • Minimum bar count: 6 bars for D ≤ 20 in; 8 bars for D > 20 in per ACI 336.3R-14 §7.3 and ACI 318-19 §26.8.2.2.
  • Spiral ties or hoops: Use #3 spiral at 6 in pitch or #4 ties at 12 in spacing per ACI 318-19 §25.7. Confinement steel is not designed in this calculator — provide per ACI requirements.
  • This calculator does not check: concrete compressive capacity of shaft, lateral load/moment capacity, or slenderness effects. Verify separately if applicable.
  • Pier concrete: Minimum f'c = 3,000 psi per IBC 2021 §1810.3.5. Typical is 4,000–5,000 psi.
