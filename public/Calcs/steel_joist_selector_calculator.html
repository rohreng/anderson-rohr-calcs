Engineering Notes:
  • Load combinations follow ASD per IBC/ASCE 7
  • Blue figures in SJI tables are total safe uniformly distributed load-carrying capacities (in plf)
  • Red figures are live load capacities for L/360 deflection check
  • Joist self-weight is included in capacity checks but NOT added to your entered dead load; verify that your dead load input includes all structural and architectural components expected to be supported
  • Span must exist in the joist's table for K and LH series; KCS series uses moment and shear capacity formulas
  • Joists are sorted by weight (lightest first); three options (Recommended, Alt 1, Alt 2) are presented
