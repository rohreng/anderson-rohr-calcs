This calculator: anchors cast vertically into the grouted top of the wall (embed plate / brace / ledger on top of wall), one or more anchors, shear in-plane or out-of-plane per the selector in Section 2. For a bolt cast horizontally into the wall FACE (single-angle ledger shear connection), use the Masonry Anchor Bolt (Shear Connection) calculator.

Continuous: enter ba, bv per foot and fastener spacing S (§3). Discrete: enter total group tension/shear and the anchor layout (rows × columns, §3).
Out-of-plane: shear perpendicular to the wall face (e.g. a wind/seismic reaction pushing the connection off the wall line) — masonry breakout is a half-cone toward the near face at lbe = b/2. In-plane: shear along the wall length (brace / diaphragm drag) — the free edge that can break out is the wall end, not the wall face.
