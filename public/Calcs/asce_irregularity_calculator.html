Notes and References

Irregularity Types per ASCE 7-16:

  • • Type 1a: Soft Story (stiffness < 70% above or < 80% avg of 3 above)
  • • Type 1b: Extreme Soft Story (stiffness < 60% above or < 70% avg of 3 above)
  • • Type 2: Mass > 150% of adjacent story
  • • Type 3: Dimension > 130% of adjacent story
  • • Type 4: In-plane discontinuity (manual check required)

Calculation Notes:

  • • Stiffness calculated as K = F/δ (Force/Deflection)
  • • Seismic Weight = Dead Load + applicable Live Load portion (see ASCE 7-16 §12.7.2)
  • • Lateral Strength = Total capacity of all SFRS elements at that level:
  • - Moment Frames: ΣMp (plastic moment capacities)
  • - Braced Frames: ΣPn (axial capacities of braces)
  • - Shear Walls: ΣVn (shear capacities)
  • • Type 5a: Weak Story (strength < 80% of story above)
  • • Type 5b: Extreme Weak Story (strength < 65% of story above)
  • • Type 4 irregularity requires manual evaluation of in-plane offsets
