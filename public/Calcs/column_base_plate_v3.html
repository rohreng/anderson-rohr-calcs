ⓘ Calculation Notes & Assumptions
  1. Concrete checks per ACI 318-19 Chapter 17 (Anchoring to Concrete). Uncracked concrete assumed throughout (ψc,N = 1.25 tension; ψc,V = 1.40 shear). φ = 0.70 Condition B (no supplementary reinforcement). Cast-in headed anchors.
  2. Embedment depth hef = 8″ default (editable per row). Standard F1554 Gr.36 headed anchor rod bearing on heavy hex nut. Pullout bearing area Abrg calculated from heavy hex nut geometry.
  3. Column centered in footing. Concrete edge distance cedge = distance from footing face to nearest anchor bolt center. Minimum 18″ default; increase per row as required. Side-face blowout not applicable when ca1 ≥ 0.4hef.
  4. Bolt gauge sg = bolt-to-bolt spacing (used for breakout cone area ANc). Auto-sized from column & plate; editable. Assumes 2-row anchor pattern (n/2 bolts per row).
  5. Steel capacity: F1554 Gr.36, Fut = 58 ksi. φ = 0.75 tension (AISC 360-22 §J3.6); φ = 0.65 shear. Pryout kcp = 2.0 when hef ≥ 2.5da (ACI 318-19 §17.7.3).
  6. Bearing: A2/A1 = 4 assumed (pedestal 2× plate each direction). Fyp = 36 ksi plate.
  7. Concrete breakout based on AISC DG1 (2nd Ed.) Examples 4.4 (tension) and 4.10 (shear). Pryout uses full-group tension breakout capacity as basis per ACI 318-19 §17.7.3.
