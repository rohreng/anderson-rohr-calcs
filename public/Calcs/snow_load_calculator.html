Calculation Explanations

Snow Drift Formulas:

  • • pf = Factor × Ce × Ct × Is × pg
  • • γ = MIN((0.13 × pg + 14), 30)
  • • hb = pf / γ
  • • hc = MAX((hr - hb), 0)
  • • hd = 0.43 × lu1/3 × (pg + 10)1/4 - 1.5
  • • lu taken ≥ 25 ft in the hd equation (ASCE 7 Fig. 7-9)

Joist Calculations:

  • • Perpendicular: Combined uniform + triangular loading
  • • Uniform Load = Pf × Spacing (balanced snow)
  • • Triangular Load = Pd × Spacing (drift, peak at wall)
  • • Reactions account for triangle centroid at W/3 from peak
  • • Parallel: Complex trapezoidal loading
  • • Snow varies linearly from wall to drift width
  • • Pf and Pd+Pf automatically pulled from drift analysis
