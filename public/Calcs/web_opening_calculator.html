Notes & Limitations:
  • LRFD load combos per ASCE 7-16 §2.3.1; ASD combos per §2.4.1. Gravity loads only (W=E=0 assumed).
  • Self-weight extracted from section designation (e.g. W24×55 = 55 plf = 0.055 k/ft). Uniform load (including self-weight) acts over the entire beam length (span + overhangs).
  • LRFD capacities: φb=0.90 (flexure), φv=1.00 (§G2.1, Cv1=1.0), φ=0.90 (AISC Design Guide 2 openings).
  • ASD capacities: Ωb=1.67 (flexure/AISC Design Guide 2 openings), Ωv=1.50 (§G2.1).
  • Overhangs / cantilevers: All hole and point-load positions (xo, xp) are measured from the left end of the beam (not the left support). Left support is at x = aL; right support at x = aL+L. Reactions are computed by statics. In hogging (cantilever) zones, LTB uses Lb,bot (bottom flange in compression) with Cb=1.0.
  • Beam design checks (§⑦): Flexure (AISC 360-22 §F2 LTB) and shear (§G2.1) are checked at the gross unperforated section using the maximum factored demand over the full beam length. These are beam-level checks only; web opening capacities Mm and Vm (Design Guide 2) govern locally at each hole and are checked separately in the per-hole results.
  • Deflection (§⑧): Computed numerically by double integration of M(x)/EI for the unperforated section at unfactored service loads, including the full-span UDL, all partial UDLs, and all concentrated loads. Interior span limits: L/360 (LL), L/240 (total). Cantilever tip limits: a/180 (LL), a/120 (total) per IBC Table 1604.3. Web openings increase flexibility — see AISC Design Guide 2 Ch.6.
  • Each hole is checked independently at its location. Interaction between adjacent holes not checked (see AISC Design Guide 2 §3.7b for spacing requirements).
  • For multiple holes: clear distance between holes must be ≥ larger of the two adjacent hole lengths (AISC Design Guide 2 §3.7b). Verify manually.
  • Partial uniform loads: Each partial UDL (wD, wL) is factored using the same governing ASCE 7-16 combo as the full-span load. Positions x1 and x2 are measured from the left end of the beam. Partial loads contribute to reactions and are fully integrated into M(x) and V(x) at each hole, into beam-level checks, and into service-level deflection.
  • Deflection at openings not computed (see AISC Design Guide 2 Ch.6). Composite action and reinforced openings not covered.
  • Section must be compact (Table B4.1b) and Fy≤65 ksi per AISC Design Guide 2 §3.1.
